# Privacy Policy for Yes Chef

## What Yes Chef Does

Yes Chef analyzes grocery receipt photos using AI to suggest recipes. All your data stays on your device.

## What We Collect

### Photos
- Receipt photos you take with camera or upload from photo library
- Used only to extract ingredients via OpenAI or Google AI
- Processed images stored locally on your device in SQLite database

### AI Conversations
- Your chat history with the recipe assistant
- Stored locally on your device
- Only sent to AI services (OpenAI/Google) when actively generating responses

### Device Information
- File timestamps, user defaults, disk space, system boot time (required by iOS APIs)
- No analytics or tracking

## Third-Party AI Processing

When you scan receipts or chat with the assistant, we send data to:

**OpenAI** (gpt-4o, gpt-4o-mini models)
- Privacy: https://openai.com/privacy

**Google AI** (Gemini models)
- Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Your receipt images and prompts are processed temporarily by these services. Review their privacy policies for details.

## Data Storage

- **Local-first:** Everything stored on your device using SQLite and MMKV
- **No cloud sync:** We don't upload your data to our servers
- **No accounts:** No registration required
- **Works offline:** Except when using AI features

## Your Controls

- **Revoke camera:** iOS Settings > Yes Chef > Camera
- **Revoke photos:** iOS Settings > Yes Chef > Photos
- **Delete all data:** Uninstall the app
- **Stop AI processing:** Don't scan receipts or use chat features

## Children's Privacy

Not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect their data.

## Legal

- We don't sell your data
- California (CCPA) and EU (GDPR) users have rights to access, delete, and port their data
- Data sent to AI services may be processed outside your country
- Uninstalling the app deletes all local data

By using Yes Chef, you agree to this policy and AI processing of your receipt photos.
