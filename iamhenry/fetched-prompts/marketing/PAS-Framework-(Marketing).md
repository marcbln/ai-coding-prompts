### PAS Framework Overview
- **Problem**: Clearly state the core issue your audience faces, making it specific and relatable to hook attention immediately.
- **Agitate**: Amplify the emotional or practical consequences of the problemâ€”stir up frustration, fear, or discomfort to heighten stakes without manipulation.
- **Solution**: Present your product/service as the straightforward fix that resolves the pain, backed by proof, benefits, or a clear call to action.

### When to Use PAS
Apply during top-of-funnel (TOFU) or interruption marketing moments, especially when addressing acute pain points or creating urgency. Ideal for impulse decisions, launches, or cold outreach to audiences feeling immediate frustration.

### Where to Use PAS
- Short, high-impact formats like social media ads
- email subject lines
- headlines
- cold DMs
- Twitter/X posts
- Avoid long-form content where agitation might overwhelm.

### Why Use PAS
It taps into loss aversion and emotional triggers from behavioral psychology, making problems feel urgent to spike interest and conversions. Can increase click-through rates by 2-5x by mirroring how people seek quick relief from discomfort.
